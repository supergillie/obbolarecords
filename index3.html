Checklist Notes
Dietary Modifications
Balanced Diet: Lean proteins, whole grains, fruits, veggies, healthy fats
Caloric Intake: Determine BMR for guided daily intake
Limit Processed Foods: Reduce intake of processed items and sugary drinks
Hydration: Drink ample water daily
Moderation: Enjoy foods in moderate amounts
Physical Activity
Cardiovascular Exercise: Walking, cycling, swimming
Strength Training: 2-3 times a week
Flexibility and Balance: Yoga or Pilates
Consistency: 150 minutes of moderate or 75 minutes of vigorous aerobic activity weekly
